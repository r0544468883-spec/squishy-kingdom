# SquishyAdi — Full Spec from Gemini Conversation

## 1. Vision & UX — Kingdom Tour Experience
- The platform is NOT a standard catalog store
- It's an immersive, interactive TOUR through a kingdom
- Adi (the kid) is the virtual TOUR GUIDE leading kids and parents through different "stations"
- Design language: Queen of Hearts from Alice in Wonderland
- Color palette: Deep royal red, rich gold. Strong dominant colors but NOT neon or glowing
- Clean line that doesn't overwhelm the eye

## 2. THE SQUISHI Feature (Core Mechanic)
- Products shown FIRST as mysterious shadow/silhouette only
- On CLICK: interactive popup reveals the full product image
- Popup includes: full image, experiential description, sale price
- This is the GAMIFICATION element integrated into the kingdom tour

## 3. Kids Interface (TikTok Display Mode)
- Products displayed as SHORT LOOP VIDEOS (demo of squishing, ASMR effect)
- "Add to cart" button must be LARGE, COLORFUL, and PROMINENT
- Kid-friendly throughout

## 4. Adults Interface & Quick Checkout
- Simple Hebrew checkout suitable for grandparents
- Payment: Bit, PayBox, credit cards (Tranzila)
- Shipping: delivery to home OR self-pickup from central Tel Aviv

## 5. "Share to Parent/Grandparent" WhatsApp System
- Unique interactive component in the shopping cart
- Child clicks "Aba/Savta, buy for me!" button
- System generates automatic WhatsApp link sent to adult with the ready-to-pay cart

## 6. Abandoned Cart Recovery (Make.com)
- If customer entered initial details and didn't complete purchase
- After exactly 1 hour: automatic WhatsApp reminder message
- "Hey! Adi's squishies are waiting for you in the kingdom. Click here to complete your order"

## 7. AI Chatbot
- Hebrew-speaking intelligent bot (Prophane AI or equivalent)
- Integrated directly in website and WhatsApp chat
- Part of the kingdom tour experience
- Identifies FAQs, recommends popular products, assists in closing deals

## 8. Marketing Strategy
- Brand: SquishyAdi / עדי סקוויש וטרנדים
- Concept: "Adi's Secret Trend Store"
- Content: 3 TikTok videos/day (unboxing, squeeze tests, behind-the-scenes packing)
- Launch on Thursdays afternoon (weekend timing)
- First circle: WhatsApp neighborhood groups in Tel Aviv
- Second circle: National sales via TikTok audience

## 9. Gamification Features

### 9a. Slide-over Cart (Royal Scroll)
- Cart slides from side like a royal parchment scroll
- Keeps child inside the kingdom tour, not redirected to gray checkout page

### 9b. Hover Effects (Hover Cards)
- Tailwind animations (hover:scale-105, hover:shadow-xl)
- Golden halo "breathing" animation around products on hover
- Enhances magical feeling

### 9c. Sticky Magic Nav
- Bottom or top navigation bar that follows user
- Contains "Kingdom Map" and quick access to new SQUISHI silhouettes

### 9d. Royal Treasure Chest (Mystery Box Upsell)
- Appears just before payment in cart
- "Sealed treasure chest" addable to cart for 15-20 NIS
- Unknown product inside (surprise element)
- Animated locked chest in cart

### 9e. Gamified Progress Bar
- Visual progress bar at top of cart, game-style
- Milestones: "You need 25 more NIS to become a 'Knight of the Kingdom' and win a surprise gift!"
- Level names: Guard → Knight → Prince/Princess → King/Queen

### 9f. Golden Hourglass (FOMO Timer)
- Tailwind-designed timer on specific products
- "The kingdom gates close in 15 minutes for this product!"
- Accelerates decision making

### 9g. Dynamic Bundling ("Kingdom Friends")
- When user reveals THE SQUISHI shadow and clicks "add to cart"
- Small Toast popup: "The squishy is bored alone in the castle! Take him this Nido for 20% off"
- Cross-sell that dramatically improves net profit margins

### 9h. WhatsApp Triggers (Make.com/n8n)
- Beyond abandoned cart recovery
- "Magic hours" — automation sending WhatsApp to past customers:
  "The kingdom opened for 2 hours only with a secret new trend. Come see the silhouette"

## 10. Playables — Playing with Product Before Buying

### 10a. Virtual Squish (Interactive Product Display)
- Instead of static image, product responds to mouse/touch
- Child can "stretch", "squish", "press" the virtual squishy on screen
- Visual feedback of squishing + popping sound

### 10b. The Playground Area (Dream Collection)
- Dedicated area where kids drag different products
- Place them next to each other, build virtual "dream collection"
- Can send collection via WhatsApp directly to parents for approval

## 11. Pop-up Games

### 11a. Queen's Magic Wheel (Kids' Wheel of Fortune)
- Pop-up game styled in Alice in Wonderland theme
- NOT "pushing" a sale but inviting to experience
- Spin to win: "magic potion" (benefit like: "free shipping on dragon's back", "mini-nido gift", "golden key for 10% off")

### 11b. Kingdom Hide & Seek (Treasure Hunt)
- Classic stay-and-play game creating deep site exploration
- Adi announces in small popup: "I hid 3 rare golden squishies across the kingdom!"
- Kids roam between category pages looking for icons
- Finding all opens "secret room" for exclusive product purchase

### 11c. Palace Maze (The Royal Maze)
- Miniature game based on touchscreen or virtual joystick (D-pad)
- Child navigates a small squishy silhouette through a kingdom-of-hearts-themed maze
- Goal: collect "gold coins" and reach palace door before time runs out (hourglass creates urgency)
- Reward: exclusive "princess squishy" available only through game, or key that opens "treasure chest" for free with purchase above minimum

### 11d. Smart Trigger for Maze
- Don't interrupt user already at payment stage
- Maze pops up in strategic situations:
  - Exit intent detected
  - Extended time on site without adding to cart
- Re-engages child/parent and creates renewed sale opportunity

## 12. Retention & WhatsApp Community

### 12a. Quiet WhatsApp Broadcast Channel ("Kingdom Ambassadors")
- Admins-only posting group
- Adi posts once a week: short video or "sneak peek" of next trend
- Direct link to website
- Creates VIP exclusive feeling, prevents churn

### 12b. Personal WhatsApp Automations (Make/n8n)
- Customer journey messages
- "Thank you for purchase" including UGC request:
  "Your squishy is on the way! Film an unboxing video for TikTok, tag us, and get a surprise on next purchase"

### 12c. Classic Email List (Parents Only)
- Email only for paying parents
- Invoices, shipping status updates
- Periodic mailings around holidays (Passover bundles, Black Friday)

## 13. SEO & GEO
- Next.js SEO techniques
- GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)
- Goal: when AI engines asked to recommend "trend toys", they source "Adi's Kingdom" as leading brand

## 14. User-Generated Content Loop
- Physical package + post-purchase emails include incentive:
  "Tag Adi's Kingdom on TikTok when opening the treasure box, get a golden key (coupon) for next order"
- Fuels organic content with minimal effort

## 15. Net Profit KPI Tracking
- Built-in smart dashboard measuring net profitability in real-time
- Weighs procurement costs (COGS) against all benefits and cross-sells
- Ensures strategy not only increases cart but consistently improves net profit

## 16. Micro-Retail (O2O — Online to Offline)

### 16a. Live Pop-up Map ("The Wandering Kingdom")
- Interactive component on website showing WHERE today's pop-up stands are
- Example: flashing red-gold marker "Today at 4PM — Adi's popup lands in the park!"

### 16b. Royal QR Scan (QR to Digital)
- Every physical stand has branded signage with QR code
- Kids scan → enter digital kingdom tour → receive "gold coin" (coupon) for next online purchase
- Turns every popup into a user/traffic acquisition machine

### 16c. Flash WhatsApp Notifications
- Sudden messages in quiet WhatsApp group for urgency (FOMO)
- "In exactly 30 minutes we open a micro-retail stand with 20 treasure chests only! First come first served"

## Architecture Decision
- Full-Stack: Next.js + Supabase (NOT Shopify)
- Allows full freedom for micro-animations, SQUISHI mechanism, dynamic tour structure
- Development using AI tools (Claude)
- 14 working days for MVP/Beta pilot
